One stage, one community, one opportunity to see great talent all in one place! BE in the Show is a celebration of the performing arts in Bellingham. Local dancers, singers, musicians, actors, and more will grace the beautiful Mount Baker Theatre main stage for a night of entertainment featuring a variety of talent from amateurs to professionals.

  • When: Saturday, January 27th, 2018 — 6:30PM-8:30PM with an opener presented by The Dance Studio
  • What: Family friendly, teen to adult variety show
  • Where: On the Main Stage at The Mount Baker Theatre
  • Cost: Only $5 - profits donated to More About Bellingham Public Schools Foundation

Talent Showcase

At 6:30PM local dancers from The Dance Studio will kick off  the night of entertainment with a dazzling opening number.  Followed by a variety show of local talent that will grace the Mount Baker Theatre stage.   There will be raffles throughout the night for audience members to participate in and Emcees to keep you laughing.  You as the audience will get to cheer for your favorite performer and the crowd favorite will receive a cash prize!   Last year's showcase spotlighted many incredible acts including the talented and powerful band Heroes, singer songwriter Fia Vierra, dancing from The Dance Studio, Dancing for Joy and the dynamic duo, "The Boys," and the hilarious stand up of Brady Secules and so much more!
